## ðŸŽ¯ Algorithm Summary

```
FINAL SCORE = Skill Match + Engagement + Freshness + Context - Diversity

40 points: Do you have required skills?
20 points: How popular is it? (views, likes, applies)
20 points: How recent is it? (0-3 days = max)
10 points: Does location/year match you?
-10 points: Penalty for same company appearing repeatedly

Total: Up to 100 points
```
